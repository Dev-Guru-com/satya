Hello World!

Inspired by wikipedia, wikileaks, scholarpedia, & reddit, satya.wiki is to be a web-application dedicated to find the truth about claims made. It intends to organize all data available on a claim and provide a "truth framework" to analyze and dissect all information by subject experts in a transparent manner including citing all verifiable sources, evidences & proofs. It is to be based on the principles of open-source, transparency, crowd-sourcing, crowd-funding, and open-intelligence. Our aim is to present truth "as it is", free of any undue influences.
